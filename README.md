# Student Mental Health EDA

Exploratory Data Analysis on university students' mental health, academic performance, and study behavior.

## Dataset
Survey data from university students covering mental health indicators, GPA, sleep quality, and study habits.

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Depression / Anxiety / PanicAttack | Mental health symptoms (0/1) |
| StudyStressLevel | Stress level (1–5) |
| CGPA | Grade point average |
| SleepQuality | Sleep quality (1–5) |
| StudyHoursPerWeek | Study hours per week |

## Key Findings
1. Most students reported at least one mental health symptom (Depression, Anxiety, or PanicAttack)
2. High stress level (5) did not correlate with more study hours or lower CGPA
3. Depression, Anxiety, and PanicAttack tend to occur together
4. Academic Engagement had low correlation with Anxiety
5. Year 1 students had the highest rate of Depression
